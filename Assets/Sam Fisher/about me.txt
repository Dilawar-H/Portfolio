Hi, I'm Sam Fisher!

What can i tell you about me? First and foremost I am a (very) proud father to a little girl who is my greatest joy in life! I love to write, travel and surf, and these things, along with my love of nature, have inspired my greatest adventures and all my business ventures.

Most people are surprised to find out I come from a Hollywood family (my memoir will be coming out soon!), but I didn't live the life that you might imagine.

I grew up on a solar-powered farm and was raised to have an affinity for animals and the environment, and a deep understanding that life is fragile and precious, and it's imperative that we find a way to live in harmony with nature.

I also lived in London for seven years before recently re-locating back to Malibu where I now live with my wife and daughter.
